About Me

Hi! I'm Ayush Uttam, a passionate web developer and a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) student. I love creating beautiful, responsive, and user-friendly websites that solve real-world problems and deliver seamless experiences.

My journey in web development started with a curiosity about how websites work — and it soon turned into a full-blown passion. Apart from coding, I enjoy exploring tech trends, reading about AI, and helping others through what I’ve learned. My goal is to become a skilled, job-ready developer who can contribute to meaningful projects and grow continuously in the tech world.
